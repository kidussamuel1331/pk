KEVIN DE BRUYNE

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Manchester City and the Belgium national team. He is widely regarded as one of the best players in the world.

De Bruyne began his football journey at a young age, starting with KVV Drongen in 1997. He later joined KAA Gent and then KRC Genk's youth academy, where he honed his skills. His professional career took off with Genk, and he eventually moved to Chelsea, Werder Bremen (on loan), Wolfsburg, and finally Manchester City.

De Bruyne's parents, Anna and Herwig, played a significant role in his development, with his mother instilling values of dedication and his father sharing his passion for football. A challenging period in his youth, involving a temporary placement with a foster family, is said to have fueled his determination and drive.

Beyond his club career, De Bruyne is a key player for the Belgium national team. He is known for his exceptional passing range, vision, and ability to score goals.
